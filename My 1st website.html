Sina Choir

  Sina choir is formed in 2008 by Abinet Kolcha and Mathewos Mabratu as kids choir. During that time, team has contained around 30 members. But officially it is named as Sina Choir
  in 2009. It was represented under kids ministry. It officially recognised as youth choir in Sep 1, 2014 E.C. Currently it holds 24 old-members and 5 new-members.
